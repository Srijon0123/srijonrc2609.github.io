Impact of Light on Human Emotion

Published on May 12, 2026 by Srijon Roy Chowdhury

This project explores how different light intensities affect emotional states using EEG signals collected from BioAmp EXG Pill and Raspberry Pi.

The system analyses brainwave variations under multiple lighting conditions and displays statistical results in real time.
